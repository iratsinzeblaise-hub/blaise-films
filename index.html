About Us

Blaise showPage Film Trading is a boutique film trading house focused on connecting independent filmmakers and rights holders with distributors, platforms and buyers worldwide. We negotiate deals for theatrical release, streaming windows, and broadcast licensing.

Mission: To find the best home and audience for every film we represent.

What we do: Rights assessment, market strategy, festival placement support, licensing negotiations, and post-sale reporting.

We offer tailored distribution strategies depending on film genre, windowing goals and target territories. Services include:

  • Negotiation of licensing agreements (theatrical, TV, streaming)
